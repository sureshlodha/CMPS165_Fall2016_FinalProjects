For-Hire Vehicles in New York For The Month of September, 2014

See a comparrison of the most popular For-Hire Vehicles Services in the Big Apple! Despite contrary belief the taxi industry remains a behemoth and the number of rides new technology like Uber/Lyft provide only make up a fraction of what the Taxi Industry provides per day.

Technology companies like Uber and Lyft need substantially more users to provide innovative reasearch and development in areas that could advance services in our daily lives. Though the Taxi Industry exerts a lot of pressure against the expansion of FHV services, they clearly reign supreme in New York for amount of riders per day.
