Top 10 National Park Visitor Count

National Parks are invaluable assets to the US Government for their reliable source and income, and analyzing tourism plays an important role in upholding that structure by giving insight so that the Department of the Interior can determine where to divert resources to.

Hover over a park name to get more information, and click on it to make the data for visitors appear on the line graph on the right.

Created by: Curtis Lee and Mengguang Zou in collaboration with Ryan Brounley, Suresh Lodha, and Shobhit Maheshwari

Created for: CMPS165, Data Programming for Visualization, Fall 2016

Description:

A geomapping of National Parks and a graph to display visitor data.
