Crime in Chicago Neighborhoods

Crime in Chicago repeatedly ranks in the top 10 in the nation. Much of that is concentrated in a small part of the city.
Many of the communities with the highest crime rates, also have the highest concentration of people living in poverty.
Fuller Park consistently has the highest crime rates at about 1 crime per 20 people, as well as about half of the community living in poverty.
