UCSC Computer Science B.S. Curriculum

Signing up for classes can be one of the most stressful experiences for a college student. Not knowing exactly what classes are available or taken at the time of signing can make the lives of students exponentially more difficult. Being able to see, in real time, what classes are available will immensely make the lives of students better.
